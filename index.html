about

Welcome to Lingjie Wan, I'd love for you to get to know me a little better and discover what inspires the work that I do.


I am a student at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts + Technology (SIAT), focusing on user experience(UX) design and the use of interactive systems. With a great sense of communicating and team building. I can always play essential roles in most group works such as project manager, graphic designer on top of my strong sketching, painting and mood board creating skill.


I am always searching for visual stimulation, find beauty in the unexpected. Exploring, experiencing, and absorbing the wonders of the world fuels my creativity and ignites my desire to create truly user-centered designs.
